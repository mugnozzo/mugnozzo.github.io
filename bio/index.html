Alek Mugnozzo - bio

Hey there! I'm Alek, a programmer and 3D generalist deeply passionate about:

  • Linux and FLOSS: I'm a supporter and user of free and open-source software.
  • Blender: As a 3D generalist (and as a programmer too), I like using Blender, the free and open-source 3D creation suite, both for work and personal projects. I model and script in Python. I also teach Blender as a Blender Italia Accredited Trainer.
  • Languages: I'm fascinated by languages and I love learning new ones and exploring different cultures through them.
  • Music: I love music. I listen a lot of it and I play it. I physically play instruments (mainly trumpet and flute) but I also like a lot to create music through code.
  • Nature Walks: I find peace and rejuvenation while walking, especially when I'm surrounded by the beauty of nature.
  • Travel: I love to travel, experiencing new cultures, and testing my language skills.
